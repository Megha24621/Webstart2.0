Established in 1973, Seshadripuram College quickly attained a prominent position among the limited number of undergraduate colleges in Bangalore at that time. In these 50 years, the number of degree colleges in Bangalore has shown astronomical growth but Seshadripuram College continues to maintain its reputation and position. From a meagre strength of thirty students at inception, the student strength has grown substantially to more than 3000 with a commensurate expansion of the faculty.
The Institution is first among the affiliated College under Bangalore University to be granted PG courses and to offer the BBA course.
